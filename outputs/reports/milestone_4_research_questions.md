# Research questions enabled by Milestone 4

These are candidate questions only. No question is selected and no result is interpreted here.

- How do attacking-action rates vary conditionally across leading, drawing, and trailing states?
- How do classic PPDA components and provider Pressure intensity vary across score states?
- Are event-level and sequence-level regain associations similarly patterned across match states?
- How do pass direction, length, and directness vary across score and numerical states?
- How stable are score-state associations across fixed windows and homogeneous state segments?
- How sensitive are associations to red-card exclusions, extra time, and mixed-state windows?
- Do team-clustered and match-clustered uncertainty estimates lead to materially different
  uncertainty widths?
- How does event-derived possession exposure compare with possession-count exposure for
  rate construction?
- Which tactical metrics are most sensitive to minimum-denominator reliability thresholds?
- How does incomplete StatsBomb 360 coverage constrain any later contextual extension of these
  state-dependent analyses?

Any later study must pre-register its primary question and specifications before inspecting or
selecting estimates.
